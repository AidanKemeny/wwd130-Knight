The Descent

(Rated R) (Scary Level: 9)

I was genuinely scared watching this movie. You will be screaming at the characters for their decisions almost all the way through, and the ending might make you mad. A group of girls decide to venture down into a cave and are met with some interesting creatures, to say the least. The feelings invoked felt like I was watching the movie Fall again. This movie is worth the watch if you want to be scared at night and never go into a cave again.
